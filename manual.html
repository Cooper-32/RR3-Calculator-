Daily Reward: Shows the daily login reward. Changes every day.

Earnings: Enter the earnings per lap.

Bonusday: Enter the Bonusday reward (+70% Fame = 70).

Distance: Enter the distance you want to drive.

Distance/Lap: Enter the distance per lap.

Money: Enter the amount of R$ or fame that you want to get.

Manager/Agent: Check if you want to hire the manager and agent.

Calculate Distance: Lets you calculate how far you have to drive for the amount of R$ or fame that you set in "Money"

Tip: For a more accurate result, enter the distance/lap you actually drive per lap (off-track shortcuts).

Example Porsche Testtrack: Distance: ~2,2km, with cuts: ~2,1km

Clean racing bonus and friends bonus are not included!
